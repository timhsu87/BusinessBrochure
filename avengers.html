Avengers Infinity War

Release date: April 27, 2018 (Canada)

8.6/10 IMDb

2018 Action/Adventure 2h 40m

Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk and the rest of the Avengers unite to battle their most powerful enemy yet -- the evil Thanos. On a mission to collect all six Infinity Stones, Thanos plans to use the artifacts to inflict his twisted will on reality. The fate of the planet and existence itself has never been more uncertain as everything the Avengers have fought for has led up to this moment.


Directors: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Box office: 2.047 billion USD

Writers: Christopher Markus, Stephen McFeely

Production Co: Marvel Studios


Intense and grande, Avengers: Infinity War is worthy of great respect. Marvel heroes from all walks of life, from Thor to Spiderman, get caught up in Thanos' scheme to cull the universe of half of its life and no one is safe. And still it manages to share a laugh, to make us smile, and in end have the viewer feel great empathy for the heroes' struggle.


The villain is complex, the protagonists vulnerable. It just looks great, it moves well with no boring parts, and I don't know if it could stuff any more characters in it. Utterly charming, I loved it.
