Mission Impossible Fallout

Release date: 27 July 2018 (USA)

8/10 IMDb

2018 Action 2h 28m

Ethan Hunt and the IMF team join forces with CIA assassin August Walker to prevent a disaster of epic proportions. Arms dealer John Lark and a group of terrorists known as the Apostles plan to use three plutonium cores for a simultaneous nuclear attack on the Vatican, Jerusalem and Mecca, Saudi Arabia. When the weapons go missing, Ethan and his crew find themselves in a desperate race against time to prevent them from falling into the wrong hands.


Director: Christopher McQuarrie

Box office: 791.1 million USD

Writers: Tom Cruise, Christopher McQuarrie, Jake Myers, J.J. Abrams

Production Co: Paramount Pictures (presents) Skydance Media (presents) TC Productions


Mission Impossible Fallout is the moment that this franchise has exceeded its inspiration: the James Bond series, in term of quality and consistency. After two decades producing this franchise, Tom Cruise has found the magic formula: give them incredible set pieces, mix in with loads of plot twist, great characterization, mix in the charisma and daredevil antics of its main star and..voila... the best Mission Impossible yet and one of the best film of the year
